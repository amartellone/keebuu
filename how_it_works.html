HOW IT WORKS?

Keebuu gives people the tools they need to buy things "socially" with friends, family, their communities, and the general public.

Shout! (aka a "deal")

When people want to buy something, they simply share the things they want to buy and hope to aggregate a buying community. It easy to buy stuff with your friends:

User create a personal shout based on:

  1. Open a deal simply sharing a URL of the thing you want to buy and use
  2. Friends, family members, friends of friends are invited via email, facebook, social media to visit the deal shared
  3. Accept people request of invitation based on users location/proximity, block or city they live
  4. Collect money
  5. When done close the deal, buy the thing and start sharing it with our buying community! A new experience as just began!
